Gingerbread Men Cookies

Gingerbread Man cookies is a popular Christmas treats that many families bake before or after the Christmas holidays. The history of the gingerbread man cookies dates back to ancient Greece and Egypt, where Gingerbread man was used for ceremonial purposes. Later on in the 16th century, the Gingerbread man recipe had replaced breadcrumbs with flour,added eggs and sweetners which resulted the cookie to be a lighter product. The first gingerbread man was credited to Queen Elizabeth 1, who she knocked the socks off visiting diginitaries in which she presented each ofthe gingerbread cookies to them based on what they like.
